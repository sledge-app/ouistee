Synchronization

Synchronization.

Synchronize your data over your local network between Ouistee for Mac and Ouistee for iPad. Ouistee for Mac will act as a syntonization server.

Hello

Requirement : Your Mac and your iPad must be connected to the same network.

A) On the iPad side

  • Click on « synchronize » on the dashboard top bar. (image A1).
  • The sync screen opens up.
  • Your iPad is now ready for the sync and available in your local network.

B) On the Mac side

  • Click on « synchronize » on the dashboard top bar. (image B1).
  • The sync screen opens up.
  • On the side list you should find all the available version of Ouistee for iPad ready for sync.
  • Click on the name of your iPad. (image C1).
  • The list of all the collections available in each device appears, with the sync status : (image C2) 
  • If the Collection does not exist on the iPad, it will be copied (left to right arrow).
  • If the Collection does not exist on the Mac, it will be copied (right to left arrow)
  • If the collection does exist on both, it will be synchronized (rollover arrows)
  • The checkbox allow to disabled the sync for the corresponding collection (image C3). 
  • Once you are ready, click on « Start ». (image C4)

Once the process is done, you can close the sync panel in the iPad, the collections are now up to date on both sides.
