LittleClock

Learn to read the hour !

Help children to learn to read the hour. You can move both hands on the clock. When moving the hands, nights and days will come into the landscape. You can also move the sun and the moon to change the hour on the clock.

This is not a game but a teaching aid.

You can switch from AM/PM mode to 24h mode from the top left button.
