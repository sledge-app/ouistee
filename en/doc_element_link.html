Link Element

Link Element.

This Element is dedicated to hyperlink (http://).

Hello

Once the Element has been dropped, go to the Data Mode (« End Editing » on the menu bar).

  • 1) To enter a hyperlink click on the pen icon at the right.
  • 2) You can enter your link in the textfield that appears.
  • 3) Once it’s done, click on the check icon on the right. 
  • 4) The hyperlink becomes active (you can click it to browse the Web) and, if available, the favicon of the website appress on the left.
